How to classify

Kubrow: five normal breeds, Kubrow body, three inherited fur colours, and an eye/energy colour.

Helminth Charger: Charger breed/body with Base, Secondary, Tertiary, Accents and Energy appearance channels.

Hybrid: record both the inherited breed and visible body. Then record what the fourth visible channel actually controls. Do not assume all hybrids behave identically.

Community research status: hybrid appearance behaviour is not fully documented by Digital Extremes. Records should therefore describe the observed UI and screenshot rather than claiming a universal inheritance rule.
