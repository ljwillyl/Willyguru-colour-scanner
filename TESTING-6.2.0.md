# Version 6.2.0 testing checklist

1. Open `index.html`; confirm the new top navigation appears.
2. On a phone-width screen, confirm the five-button bottom navigation appears.
3. Open Scanner and perform a normal scan.
4. Sign into My Kennel and confirm existing records load.
5. Open Marketplace and confirm listings and Sell a Kubrow still work.
6. Open DNA Explorer and confirm filters/results work.
7. Confirm Statistics loads.
8. Check browser console for errors.
9. Test at widths near 360 px, 768 px, and desktop.
10. Hard refresh after deployment to clear old cached CSS/JS.
